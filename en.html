HOW WE WORK?

  • STEP 1. CONSULTING

    During the initial consultation phase, questions arise about the intricacies of conducting businesses in various fields. Our experts are ready to provide detailed answers and recommendations for a successful start. We will answer it.

  • STEP 2. IMPORT AND EXPORT

    At this stage, we discuss the needs and goals of your import business. After analyzing goods, we begin the search for optimal suppliers and develop a logistics strategy. Regarding the identification of the expansion market for the export product category, we establish partnerships, create effective marketing strategies, and analyze product requirements and standards.

  • 3 STEPS. CONTRACT SIGNING

    Upon receiving your request, we provide information about potential suppliers and products according to your requirements.

  • 4 STEPS. : CONTROL AND PREPARATION FOR SHIPMENT

    Quality control is ensured before shipping the goods. Product checks are conducted at all stages, with photo and video documentation of the process. 10% of the products are randomly checked to guarantee compliance with standards.

  • 5 STEPS. FROM HAND TO HAND

    After the products are shipped, we take control and ensure reliable delivery according to your chosen tariff. Our goal is to make your business process with China convenient and reliable.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS TRADE AND INVESTMENTS

  • What are the consultation terms?

    We will contact you after registering your request and arrange for an online consultation via platforms such as WeChat, WhatsApp, or Telegram.

  • What is the payment structure for your services?

    Our fixed commission is 10-15% of the purchased item's value, but not less than $1000 (USD).

  • How can I pay for the purchased goods?

    Payment can be made through a bank transfer or in cash after the contract is signed.

  • How is the international shipping payment handled?

    International shipping payment is made in dollars and in the customer's currency at the exchange rate upon receiving the cargo.

  • What procedures are in place in case of loss of goods?

    In the event of cargo loss and an insurance claim, financial compensation is provided based on the insured amount. Branded copies are not provided.

  • Do you provide certificates?

    We prepare certificates and declarations in accordance with the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan and offer the option to process necessary documents.
